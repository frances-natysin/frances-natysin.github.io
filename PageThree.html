Ingredients and Preparation

Ingredients:

  • Lamb or goat meat (main protein)
  • Jameed (fermented dried yogurt)
  • Rice or bulgur
  • Flatbread (shrak or markook)
  • Toasted almonds or pine nuts
  • Salt, black pepper, cinnamon(nationaldish.world)

Preparation Steps:

  1. Soak and blend jameed to create a smooth yogurt sauce.
  2. Slow-cook the lamb in the jameed sauce until tender.
  3. Place flatbread on a large platter and spread rice over it.
  4. Pour meat and sauce over rice.
  5. Garnish with toasted nuts for flavor and texture(jordannews.jo).

“The act of preparing and serving Mansaf is a communal effort that reinforces social bonds”

- jordannews.jo

Preparation Tips:

  • Jameed can be soaked overnight for smoother sauce.
  • Lamb is often marinated with spices before cooking for richer flavor.
  • Rice is sometimes cooked in meat broth for additional aroma.
